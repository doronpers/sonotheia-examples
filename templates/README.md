# Templates

This directory contains reusable templates for tracking and documenting your work.

## Available Templates

### [learning-journal.md](learning-journal.md)

A comprehensive template for tracking your AI-assisted development sessions.

**Use this template to:**
- Document problem definitions and success criteria
- Track AI prompts and responses
- Record what worked and what didn't
- Identify patterns over time
- Improve your AI collaboration skills

**When to use it:**
- During or after development sessions
- When learning new AI tools or patterns
- To track recurring issues
- For post-mortems and retrospectives

## How to Use Templates

1. **Copy the template** to your working location
2. **Fill it out** during or immediately after your session
3. **Review periodically** to identify patterns
4. **Share insights** with your team (anonymize as needed)
